Obituary

Lisa R. Metzler, age 37, of Lancaster, PA, passed away unexpectedly on Saturday, March 14, 2026. Lisa will be remembered most for her kind, empathetic heart and her unwavering devotion to those she loved. She had a profound connection to animals, particularly her cherished cat, Sapphire.

Throughout her life, Lisa found great joy in the simple beauties of the world—the warmth of the sun during beach trips with her mother and the joyful anticipation of her favorite Christmas cookies made by her sister.

A woman of vibrant spirit, Lisa loved to sing with a powerful and sincere voice. Her innocence and infectious laughter were hallmarks of her personality, leaving a lasting impression on everyone she met. Though she had no children of her own, her nieces and nephews knew her as a true friend and a constant source of support.

Lisa fought a courageous battle with addiction. Her family shares this truth to break the silence and stigma surrounding substance use disorder, with the hope that it may encourage others to seek the help they need.

Surviving Family

She is survived by her mother, Karen K. Fleming and husband Ronald; her father, Gerald A. Metzler and wife Susan; and her siblings: Kelly (Stephen) Biddison, Jeremy (Casey) Metzler, Amy (Sam) Mindlin, and Greg DeMascolo. She is also survived by a large, loving extended family of nieces and nephews.

If you wish to honor Lisa's memory, the family kindly asks that donations be made to Overdose Lifeline to support those struggling with addiction and to help prevent further loss.
